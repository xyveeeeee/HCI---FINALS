Breakfast

Tuna Scrambled Egg

Servings: 1

A tuna scrambled egg is a dish where scrambled eggs are combined with tuna, typically canned tuna. The tuna is usually mixed into the eggs while they’re cooking, creating a savory, protein-packed dish. You might also add extra ingredients like onions, cheese, herbs, or vegetables to enhance the flavor. It’s a quick, nutritious meal that’s often enjoyed for breakfast.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 whole canned tuna
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup of chopped red onions
  • 2 tablespoon of olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Salt and Black Pepper for taste

Instructions

  1. Firstly, add olive oil or any vegetable oil to the cooking pan.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add chopped red onion and stir for a few minutes till it is cooked.
  3. Add canned tuna into the pan and give a stir for few more minutes. Make sure you don't include the water from the tuna, for this will cause the oil to splatter.
  4. Add the beating egg to the pan and stir to mix with the tuna.
  5. Lastly, add salt and black pepper for taste, and stir again before turning off the heat.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
