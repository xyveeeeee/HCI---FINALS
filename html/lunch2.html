Tuna Lettuce Wrap

Servings: 3

A tuna salad lettuce wrap is a healthy and low-carb alternative to a traditional tuna salad sandwich. It involves wrapping a mixture of tuna salad (typically made with tuna, mayo, mustard, and seasonings) in large, crisp lettuce leaves instead of bread.

Ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 1 (6 ounce) can chunk light tuna, drained
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon light mayonnaise
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 leaves green lettuce, rinsed and patted dry
  • 2 tablespoons finely shredded low-fat Cheddar cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine tuna, mayonnaise, onion, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Lay lettuce leaves out. Divide the tuna mixture among the leaves, placing it in the middle of each leaf.
  3. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over the tuna (optional).
  4. Roll up the lettuce leaves to create wraps.
