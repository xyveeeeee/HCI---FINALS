Fried Egg Sandwich

Servings: 1

A fried egg sandwich is a simple and delicious dish typically made by placing a fried egg (or multiple eggs) between slices of bread. The bread is usually toasted or lightly buttered for extra flavor. Sometimes, the sandwich can be enhanced with other ingredients, like cheese, bacon, avocado, lettuce, tomato, or condiments such as mayonnaise or hot sauce.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 1 Slice of Cheese
  • 1 Tablespoon Mayonnaise
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook Eggs:
    • Fry or scramble eggs until cooked through.
  2. Prepare Bread:
    • Lightly toast sliced bread (optional).
  3. Assemble:
    • Spread mayonnaise on one slice of bread. Add cooked eggs and cheese. Top with the other slice of bread.
