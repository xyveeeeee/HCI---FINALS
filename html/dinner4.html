Dinner

Grilled Tilapia

Servings: 2

Grilled tilapia is a dish made by cooking tilapia fish on a grill. Tilapia is a mild-flavored, white-fleshed fish that is commonly used in cooking because of its delicate taste and versatile texture. When grilled, tilapia typically has a slightly crispy exterior while maintaining a tender, flaky interior.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 to 2 pieces large tilapia, cleaned and gutted
  • 2 pieces tomato, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Make the Filling:
    • Mix diced tomato, onion, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  2. Stuff the Tilapia:
    • Carefully fill the tilapia with the mixture.
  3. Coat the Fish:
    • Combine oil and salt, brush onto the tilapia.
  4. Wrap & Grill:
    • Wrap the tilapia in foil. Grill for 12 minutes per side.
  5. Serve:
    • Enjoy the grilled tilapia with eggplant salad and rice.
