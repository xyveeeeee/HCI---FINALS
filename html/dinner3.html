Chili Garlic Tofu

Servings: 3

Chili garlic tofu is a flavorful dish made with tofu that’s stir-fried or sautéed in a spicy and savory chili garlic sauce. The tofu is typically cubed and cooked until crispy or golden on the outside, while maintaining a soft interior. The sauce is usually made from ingredients like chili paste, garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and sometimes other seasonings like ginger or sesame oil.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 450 grams firm or extra firm tofu (around 3.5 cups tofu cubes)
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3-4 spring onion stalks finely chopped, keep the green and white parts separated
  • 2.5 tablespoons chili oil
  • 1 tablespoon oyster mushroom sauce or any other Asian stir-fry sauce of your choice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil or canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar (white sugar can also be used)
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preparation:
    • Press tofu with a weight for 15 minutes.
    • Cut tofu into cubes.
  2. Marinate & Bake:
    • Toss tofu in chili oil, garlic powder, and salt.
    • Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 12-15 minutes.
  3. Sauce:
    • Sautee garlic and white onion.
    • Add brown sugar, pepper, soy sauce, and oyster sauce.
  4. Combine & Serve:
    • Add baked tofu and green onions to the sauce.
    • Garnish with more green onions.
