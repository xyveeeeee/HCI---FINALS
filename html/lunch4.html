Burger Steak Sardinas

Servings: 4

Burger Steak Sardinas is a Filipino dish that combines sardines in tomato sauce with a savory gravy sauce, similar to the popular burger steak dish, which typically uses beef patties served with gravy. In the case of Burger Steak Sardinas, the sardines replace the beef, and the tomato sauce from the sardines adds a rich, flavorful base to the gravy.

Ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans sardines in tomato sauce
  • 1 banana blossom, minced
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup cooking oil

Gravy Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Arrange the minced banana blossom in a bowl:
    • Add 1 tablespoon of salt. Rub it all over.
    • Wash the salt off and drain the excess water.
  2. Separate the sardines from the sauce:
  3. In a bowl, combine:
    • Vegetable broth
    • Soy sauce
    • Onion powder
    • Cornstarch
  4. Add the following:
    • 1 egg
    • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

    Mix everything together while mashing the sardines in the process.

  5. Gradually add all-purpose flour while mixing until the mixture becomes firm.
  6. Scoop around ¼ cup of the mixture and shape it into patties. Set aside.
  7. Make the gravy:
    • Melt the butter in a saucepan.
    • Add all-purpose flour and stir while cooking until lumps form and the mixture turns medium brown.
    • Add the sauce from the canned sardines and half of the milk while constantly stirring.
    • Add more milk as needed until your desired consistency is achieved.
    • Season with onion powder, salt, and ground black pepper. Set aside.
  8. Meanwhile, heat 2 tablespoons of cooking oil in a clean pan.
  9. Fry the burger patties on medium heat until browned. Flip and cook the opposite side.
  10. Arrange the sardines burger steak on a plate and top with the gravy.
  11. Serve this with rice. Share and enjoy!
