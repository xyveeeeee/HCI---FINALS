Ginisang Kangkong with Sardines

Servings: 4

Ginisang Kangkong with Sardines is a Filipino dish that combines sautéed water spinach (kangkong) with canned sardines in a savory mix of garlic, onions, and tomatoes. It's a quick and simple dish that's popular for its affordability and nutritional value. This dish is often served with steamed rice and is a staple of Filipino home cooking, especially as a quick meal option. It has a balance of salty, savory, and earthy flavors.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 bundle Water Spinach or Kangkong Leaves
  • 1 can Sardines any brand, hot or regular flavor
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 medium size onion chopped
  • 1 pc large tomato
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • pinch ground pepper
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • pinch salt
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Put the wok in a medium to low heat.
  2. Put the cooking oil
  3. Saute the garlic, onions and tomatoes
  4. Add the sardines
  5. Add the kangkong leaves
  6. Add 1 cup of water
  7. Mix it well then add the ground pepper, a pinch of salt, and soy sauce and the sugar.
  8. Taste it and adjust seasonings to your liking
  9. Let it simmer for 2 minutes
  10. Once the leaves are done and the taste is perfect to your palate. Take it off from the heat
  11. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve right away.
