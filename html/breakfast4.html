Cabbage Omelette (Tortang Repolyo)

Servings: 4

A Cabbage Omelette (Tortang Repolyo) is a Filipino dish made primarily from cabbage (repolyo) mixed with eggs and then fried into a savory omelette. It's a simple, flavorful, and budget-friendly meal that combines the crunchiness of cabbage with the richness of eggs.

Ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams (1/4 kilo) Repolyo
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ¼ chicken broth cube (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Chop the cabbage into thin strips. After chopping, wash and drain well.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the cabbage strips, eggs, and all-purpose flour.
  3. Dissolve the chicken broth cube in ½ tablespoon hot water. Add the broth mixture to the cabbage. Mix well.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Heat a little bit of oil in a pan. Scoop some cabbage mixture and fry on low heat until light brown.
    • Cover the pan while cooking.
  6. After a few minutes, flip the cabbage omelet to cook the other side. Cover again.
  7. When both sides are cooked, remove from heat. Fry the rest of the mixture.
  8. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce.
