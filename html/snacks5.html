Crispy Edamame

Servings: 5

Crispy edamame refers to edamame (young soybeans) that are cooked until they become crispy, often by roasting or frying. Typically, the edamame pods are tossed with seasonings like salt, pepper, garlic, or chili flakes, then roasted or air-fried until they have a crunchy texture.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package frozen shelled edamame (green soybeans)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven:
    • 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Thaw edamame:
    • Rinse edamame in a colander under cold water for about 5 minutes. Drain.
  3. Spread & season:
    • Spread edamame in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bake:
    • Bake for 15 minutes or until cheese is crispy and golden.
