Breakfast

Omurice

Servings: 1

Omurice (オムライス) is a popular Japanese dish that combines an omelette with fried rice. The rice is usually stir-fried with ingredients like chicken, vegetables, and ketchup, giving it a savory-sweet flavor. Once the rice is cooked, it is wrapped inside a fluffy, soft omelette. The dish is often topped with more ketchup or a savory sauce, making it a comforting, flavorful meal.

Ingredients

Ingredients:

Chicken Fried Rice:

  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 100g chicken breast, diced
  • 1/4 cup carrots, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1/4 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup (assumed to be available)

Omelette:

  • 4 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Firstly, add olive oil or any vegetable oil to the cooking pan.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add chopped red onion and stir for a few minutes till it is cooked.
  3. Add canned tuna into the pan and give a stir for a few more minutes.
    • Make sure you don't include the water from the tuna, as this will cause the oil to splatter.
  4. Add the beaten egg to the pan and stir to mix with the tuna.
  5. Lastly, add salt and black pepper for taste, and stir again before turning off the heat.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
