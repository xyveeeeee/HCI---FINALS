Dinner

Adobong Sitaw with Chicken

Servings: 4

Adobong Sitaw with Chicken is a Filipino dish that combines two classic ingredients—sitaw (string beans) and chicken—in the style of adobo, a popular Filipino cooking method. In adobo, meat or vegetables are typically simmered in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and pepper, which gives it a tangy, savory flavor.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 25 pieces of green long beans, cut into 2 inches
  • 200 grams of boneless chicken breast
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of vinegar
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped finely
  • 3 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • ½ cup of water

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan. Add chopped garlic and stir for a few seconds.
  2. Add chopped onion and stir until translucent.
  3. Add the chopped chicken breast and stir until cooked.
  4. Add the green beans and mix for a minute.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce, vinegar, and oyster sauce. Mix and then add the water. Increase the heat.
  6. Once boiling, lower the heat and simmer for 10 minutes until the green beans are cooked.
  7. Serve with rice.
