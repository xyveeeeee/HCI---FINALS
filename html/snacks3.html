Cottage Cheese Sweet Potatoes

Servings: 2

Cottage cheese sweet potatoes is a dish that combines the creamy texture and mild flavor of cottage cheese with the natural sweetness of roasted or mashed sweet potatoes. The dish can be made in various ways, but typically, it involves mixing or layering cooked sweet potatoes with cottage cheese, and sometimes additional ingredients like cinnamon, honey, or even savory seasonings like salt and pepper.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 Raw Sweet Potato
  • ¼ teaspoon Olive Oil or Avocado Oil
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • ½ cup 2% Cottage Cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Granola
  • 1 teaspoon Honey
  • Fresh Berries (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven:
    • 400°F (200°C). Use BAKE setting on toaster oven.
  2. Prep sweet potato:
    • Slice in half lengthwise, rub with oil, and place cut-side down.
  3. Bake:
    • Bake for 30 minutes or until tender. Cool slightly.
  4. Mash & top:
    • Flip, sprinkle with cinnamon, mash. Top with cottage cheese, more cinnamon, granola, and honey. Add berries if desired. Enjoy!
