Breakfast

Tofu and Tuna Scrambled Eggs

Servings: 2

Tofu and Tuna Scrambled Eggs is a savory dish that combines tofu, tuna, and scrambled eggs, offering a mix ofprotein-rich ingredients. It’s a great option for a breakfast or brunch, especially for those looking to incorporate more plant-based foods into their meals while still enjoying some animal protein.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • Firm Tofu: 200gh
  • Canned Tuna: 1 can (80g), drained
  • Eggs: 2 large
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds: 1 tablespoon (optional)
  • Green Onions: 2 tablespoons, chopped
  • Salt and Pepper: to taste
  • Soy Sauce: 1 tablespoon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tofu:
    • Wrap the tofu in a clean kitchen towel and press gently to remove excess water.
    • Crumble the tofu into small pieces into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mix Ingredients:
    • Add the drained tuna to the bowl with the crumbled tofu.
    • Crack the eggs into the bowl and whisk until well combined.
  3. Add Seasoning:
    • Stir in the chopped green onions and sesame seeds (if using).
    • Season with salt, pepper, and soy sauce (if using) to taste.
  4. Cook the Mixture:
    • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    • Pour the tofu and tuna mixture into the skillet, spreading it evenly.
    • Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are fully cooked and the mixture is heated through.
  5. Serve:
    • Once cooked, remove from heat and serve warm.
    • This dish can be enjoyed on its own or paired with steamed rice or toast.
