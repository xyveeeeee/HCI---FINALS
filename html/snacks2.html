Yogurt with Banana and Nuts

Servings: 2

Yogurt with banana and nuts is a simple and nutritious snack or breakfast option. It's a great combination of protein, fiber, healthy fats, and vitamins, offering a balanced, satisfying meal. You can adjust the flavor by adding honey, granola, or cinnamon, depending on your taste preferences.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • Yogurt(using half amount)
  • 2 pieces Banana
  • ¼ Nuts

Estimated Price

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Banana:
    • Peel the banana and slice it into rounds or chunks, depending on your preference.
  2. Assemble:
    • In a bowl, add the yogurt as the base. Top with the banana slices and sprinkle the nuts on top.
  3. Serve:
    • Mix everything together if you'd like or enjoy as separate layers for texture.
